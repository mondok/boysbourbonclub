The Boys Bourbon Club, founded in 2024, is a community of bourbon enthusiasts located in the Tampa Bay area. Our founding members, Matt Mondok, Chad Bostak, Sean Christensen, Chip Folger, Lance Sturgis, and Chris Payne, came together with a shared passion for celebrating and studying different bourbons.

Our club's mission is to enjoy one another's company, savor good food, and contribute back to the community. We believe in the art of bourbon as a craft drink and are always eager to expand our circle with new members who share this appreciation.

Whether you're a seasoned bourbon connoisseur or just beginning your journey, the Boys Bourbon Club offers a welcoming environment to explore the rich history and diverse flavors of bourbon. Join us as we raise a glass to friendship, flavor, and philanthropy.
