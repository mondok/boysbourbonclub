About Us

Our club was founded by a group of bourbon enthusiasts who share a passion for fine spirits, great food, and meaningful connections. 

We gather regularly to explore different bourbons, share tasting notes, and enjoy each other's company.

Our founding members are Matt Mondok, Chad Bostak, Sean Christensen, Chip Folger, Lance Sturgis, and Chris Payne.  

Our goal is to celebrate and study different bourbons, enjoy one another's company, enjoy good food, and contribute back to the community. We're looking to expand with new members that love bourbon as a craft drink.
